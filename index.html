The Science

Why Blade Seizure
Destroys Machines

When a blade jams against rebar, Newton's Second Law dictates the outcome. Without a mechanical clutch, impact force reaches catastrophic levels in microseconds — before any algorithm can respond.

F = MV÷T
MMass of blade & drivetrain
VOperating blade speed
TTime to full stop
FResulting impact force

When T approaches zero during blade seizure, F approaches infinity. A mechanical clutch extends T by dissipating energy as friction heat. Software cannot do this.

Vopen Super Clutch

Clutch slips at zero delay, extending effective stop time to 10–20 seconds. Force is absorbed as heat. Zero gearbox damage.

Stop Time (T)Impact ForceOutcome
1.0 sec1×Safe
0.1 sec10×Caution
0.01 sec100×Critical
0.001 sec1,000×Catastrophic

Without Mechanical Clutch

Blade seizure stops in ~0.001 sec. Gearbox absorbs 1,000× normal force. Gear destruction is immediate. Machine runs away.
