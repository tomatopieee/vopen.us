Core Technology

What Makes It Different

"Those who control time, control everything." — Mechanical protection first, smart shutdown second. Shared across all four wall saw models.

⚙️
Super Clutch System
Mechanical clutch slips at zero delay under overload — absorbing peak shock before it reaches the gearbox. Creates a 10–20 second control window. Zero gearbox damage.
💧
Full-Path Water Cooling
Cooling flows through drive unit, motor housing, clutch, gearbox, and out through the blade flange. Complete thermal management. 24h continuous operation.
⚡
One-Key Triple-Connect
Power, electrical, and water connections unified into a single quick-connect system. One cable, one water hose. Faster setup, single-operator capability.
🔧
Tool-Free Quick Gearbox
Switchable without tools for different cutting conditions. Enables full motor power output with large blades by mechanically reducing speed while doubling torque.
🛡️
Dual Buffer Feed System
Elastic buffer structures at both the feed wheel and arm tip pre-absorb mechanical shock — protecting the drive system in the most demanding conditions.
🤖
Smart Assisted Cutting
Hundreds of control calculations per second. Works with the Super Clutch to recover from jam conditions. Automatic cruise cutting mode after 1 minute.
