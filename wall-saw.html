Core Technology

What Makes It Different

"Those who control time, control everything." — Vopen's engineering philosophy: filter peak loads, create control time, maintain stable operation under any condition.

⚙️
Super Clutch System
Mechanical clutch rated to match motor output. Slips at zero delay under overload — absorbing peak shock before it reaches the gearbox. Creates a 10–20 second control window. Zero gearbox damage.
💧
Full-Path Water Cooling
Cooling flows through the drive unit, motor housing, clutch, gearbox, bearing outer rings, saw head gearbox, and finally out through the blade flange. Complete thermal management — not just local cooling.
⚡
One-Key Triple-Connect
Power, electrical, and water connections between motor and saw head are unified into a single quick-connect system. Result: one cable, one water hose. Faster setup, single-operator capability.
🔧
Tool-Free Quick Gearbox
The gearbox mounts between motor and arm — switchable without tools for different cutting conditions. Includes full water cooling. Enables full motor power output with large blades by mechanically reducing speed while doubling torque.
🛡️
Dual Buffer Feed System
Both the feed grip wheel and the arm tip use elastic buffer structures — similar to independent car suspension. Pre-absorbs mechanical shock peaks before they reach the motor, gearbox, and bearings.
🤖
Smart Assisted Cutting
Performs hundreds of control calculations per second. Works together with the Super Clutch: the clutch creates time, the algorithm uses that time. Continuously adjusts parameters to maintain stable, controlled cutting.
